Track Coordinators

Track Coordinators in an international conference manage and oversee specific thematic tracks or sessions. They are responsible for organizing speakers, reviewing submissions, and ensuring smooth execution of sessions related to their track. Their role helps maintain the quality and focus of discussions within their assigned area of expertise.
